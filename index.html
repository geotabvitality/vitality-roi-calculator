Methodology & Assumptions

All figures are derived from published industry benchmarks and Geotab Vitality program data. The table and formulas below explain every number used in your estimate.

Industry Segment Baseline Assumptions ▾
SegmentTypical Operations Miles/Yr MPG Avg Collision CostIncident Rate Turnover RateReplace Cost Gas %Diesel %EV/Hybrid % Sources

Incident Rate = at-fault commercial fleet incidents per driver/year. 50% unavoidable churn factor applied to retention savings (retirements, medical, relocation). Collision and replacement costs reflect regional differences in healthcare systems, legal liability regimes, and labor markets — not simply currency-converted from a single source. Sources: NSC/FMCSA/NHTSA (US), IBC/CASC (Canada), ETSC/Eurostat (EU), DfT/HSE (UK), BITRE/Safe Work Australia.

Calculation Formulas ▾
Fuel Savings
Drivers × (Miles ÷ MPG) × Blended fuel cost × Fuel Improvement %
Blended fuel cost = (Gas % × Gas price) + (Diesel % × Diesel price). EV/hybrid vehicles contribute $0 to blended fuel cost — naturally excluded. Fleet-type MPG and annual miles are defaults from the table above. For non-US regions, fuel prices are entered in local currency per litre and converted to per-gallon equivalent internally.
Collision Risk Savings
Drivers × Incident Rate × Avg Collision Cost × Collision Reduction %
Incident rate and collision cost are segment defaults. All-in cost per at-fault incident includes vehicle repair, medical, liability, downtime, and claims administration. Applies to all vehicle types.
Driver Retention Savings
Drivers × Turnover Rate × Retention Improvement % × 50% × Driver Replace Cost
50% unavoidable churn factor accounts for retirements, medical departures, and relocations that no program can prevent. Source: Geotab Vitality King Township case study methodology. Applies to all vehicle types.
Program Cost & ROI
Program Cost = Drivers × $15/month × 12  |  ROI = (Gross − Cost) ÷ Cost × 100
Net Savings = Gross Savings − Program Cost. Payback = Program Cost ÷ (Gross − Cost) × 12 months. $15/driver/month converted to local currency at approximate market rates for non-USD regions.
